About us

As both builder and project manager, Homeworx is recognized for creating distinctive homes with enduring value. Homeworx principal, Oliver Young and his wife and in-house designer, Lisa have over 12 years of experience in the renovation and building business. Together, their goal is to create fine homes that meet their clients’ everyday needs, providing them with an end product that is exactly what they envisioned. Oliver and Lisa’s process is both personalized and systematic, paying close attention to every detail and allowing their clients to have a stress free building experience. Oliver is a Licensed Residential Builder through the Home Owners Protection Agency and Homeworx is also pleased to be a member of the New Home Warranty Program.

Our Mission
